This is a two players game where they compete against each other
It's about attacking/slapping and defending

each player has three lives
attacker/defender role switch every 10 seconds

The attacker:
    - aims to hit the defender
    - has a time limit within where they need to attack/else loose the chance
    - three keys
        - left: attack left
        - right: attack right
        - middle: trick/fake attack/move a bit without attacking

The defender:
    - aims to not get hit and block the attack
    - can hold up hands to block damage
        - hold up hand will cost some energy per second
        - hold up two hands will cost double the amount
        - energy will resume over time when not defending
    - would not be able to defend without energy


- Two players with basic movement or idle stance
- MatchManager role switching every 10 seconds
- Attacker input: left / right / fake
- Defender input: block left / right
- Energy drain and regen
- Hit detection
- Lives and win/lose state
- Animations and UI
